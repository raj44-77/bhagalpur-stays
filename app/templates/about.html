Founder's Note

From Silk City, with love

Bhagalpur isn't just a city — it's an emotion. From the ancient ruins of Vikramshila to the serene Ganga ghats, from the world-famous Tussar silk to the aroma of Litti Chokha — this city has always welcomed travelers with open arms.

But finding a reliable hotel here was always a challenge. That's why I built Bhagalpur Stays — a platform that connects travelers to verified, trusted hotels across Silk City. No more guessing. No more uncertainty. Just honest stays, fair prices, and local support that speaks your language.

This is more than a business. It's my commitment to my city — to put Bhagalpur on India's travel map, one happy guest at a time.

— Kumar Raj

Founder, CEO & Developer · Bhagalpur Stays
